# Wolfwalkers' Journey

A fun, kid-friendly exercise app that guides children through daily physical therapy routines with a magical wolfwalker theme.

## What is this?

Wolfwalkers' Journey helps kids complete their exercise routine in a fun, engaging way. The app features:

- **9 guided exercises** including stretches, strength training, balance, and fun activities
- **Visual timers and counters** so kids know exactly what to do
- **Progress tracking** with badges, stickers, and achievements
- **Streak system** to encourage daily practice (with grace days for busy families)
- **Works offline** - no internet needed once loaded
- **Installs on your device** like a regular app

## How to use

1. Open `index.html` in any web browser
2. Follow along with the exercises shown on screen
3. Tap the big buttons to move through the routine
4. Earn stickers and badges for completing sessions!
